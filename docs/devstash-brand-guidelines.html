06 — Usage Guidelines

Do's & don'ts

These rules protect the integrity of the mark across all placements. When in doubt, refer back to the approved lockup variants.

DevStash
✓ Do
Use the approved lockup with correct colors and proportions on a dark or light background.
DevStash
✕ Don't
Change the icon background color or swap the accent colors. The mark background is always #0B0F19.
DevStash
✓ Do
On light backgrounds, use the dark wordmark (#0B0F19). The mark always keeps its dark background.
DevStash
✕ Don't
Rotate, skew, or otherwise transform the logo. It must always appear at 0° horizontal.
✓ Do
Use the icon mark alone (without wordmark) for app icons, favicons, and small square placements ≥16px.
DevStash
✕ Don't
Substitute different fonts for the wordmark. Only use DM Sans 700 for the brand wordmark.
DevStash
✓ Do
Use the monochrome white version for single-color applications like embroidery, screen printing, or overlays.
DevStash
✕ Don't
Use the logo at low opacity or over busy backgrounds. Always maintain clear contrast and legibility.
